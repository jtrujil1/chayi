About Us

The Chinese character for tea is 茶, pronounced chayi. The Hindi word for tea is चाय, pronounced chaay. The Russian word for tea is чай, pronounced chay. Across cultures, generations, and even civilizations, tea has acted as a drink that brings individuals together, a token of welcoming. Here at Chayi House, we have the same intention. Based on a culture of service, we promise to give you an experience filled with not only world-class tea, but one where you feel well-rested and reenergized.

Where are we Located?

Our teas come from all over the world: China, India, Southeast Asia, Europe, and more, but our home is in Somerville. You can find our chayi café at Davis Square in Somerville. We first open two years ago, and we are a bustling spot for college students, Boston commuters, and morning runs. We are in the works of opening new locations across MA.
